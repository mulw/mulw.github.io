Social works

The Catholic group is much more than just a place of worship. In addition to their weekly mass, bible sessions, and community service projects, the members of the group are also active in social and outreach activities.

One of their main initiatives is visiting the needy in their sorrounding community.

Through these efforts, they are able to make a tangible difference in people's lives and spread joy and kindness.

These events are open to everyone, regardless of religious background, and they provide an opportunity for people to connect and have fun together.

Through their dedication and hard work, St. Dominic became much more than just a Catholic group at Umma University.

Mission Statement:
To create a welcoming and supportive community for Catholic students at Umma University, where individuals can come together to worship, serve others, and deepen their faith.

The Catholic group at Umma University was called St. Dominic, named after the patron saint of preaching and teaching. The group help promote understanding and respect between different religions

Moments

In every end year, the members of St. Dominic come together for a special gathering to celebrate their achievements and to renew their commitment to their faith and community. This gathering is an important event for the group, and it is eagerly anticipated by everyone.

The come together is a time for the members of St. Dominic to reflect on their spiritual journey and to celebrate their accomplishments.
It is also a time of great joy and celebration.

Challenges

As they became more involved in the university, they also found ways to bridge the gap between their faith and the larger community.

They joined forces with the other students to organize interfaith events and discussions, promoting understanding and respect between different religions.

In the past, they had faced challenges and opposition, but they had also made many friends and allies.
The members of the group are confident that they would continue to make a positive impact and create a world where everyone is free to practice their faith in peace.
